## Voice Cloning Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share Your Requirements">
    Brief our team on your exact needs and the voice profile you want to create.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up & Select a Voice Actor">
    Sign up with Murf to begin the voice cloning process and choose an actor
    whose voice you want to clone or provide your own voice data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Record a Custom Script">
    Get a high-quality voice recording of a custom script by the selected voice
    actor. Ensure clarity, consistency, and a noise-free environment for the
    best results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Processing & Voice Cloning">
    Sit back while Murf’s AI processes the recordings and creates a custom AI
    voice model. The training process typically takes 1 to 4 weeks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Your Custom AI Voice">
    Once ready, unlock 24/7 access to your custom AI voice on Murf Studio. Use
    it for voiceovers, narration, and content creation at any time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Safe, Secure, and Dependable

Our systems are built to protect your data, ensure security of usage for your clone and provide exclusive access to your team for creating custom voiceovers using text to speech.

#### Data Security

Our AI models and voice data is stored in Amazon Web Services (AWS), which is compliant with PCI-DSS, SOC 1, SOC 2 and SOC 3, FedRAMP, GDPR, HIPAA/HITECH, and more. All data flowing across our servers in the AWS global network is automatically encrypted at the physical layer before it leaves the secured facilities. Service-to-service TLS connections and encrypted data transfer over HTTPS using SHA-2 compliant cipher suites ensure additional security.
